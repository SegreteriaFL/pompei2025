Pilgrimage Program

Day by day: celebrations and activities.

First day

Thursday, September 18 — Departure for Pompei

  • Morning · Departures from places of origin (bus/plane/train). Packed lunch.
  • Afternoon · Arrival in Pompei and hotel accommodation.
  • 17:30 · Gathering in front of the Sanctuary.
  • 18:15 · Greetings and plenary assembly – Theme: Getting up and going (Sala Trapani).
  • 20:00 · Dinner (Sala Signora).
  • 21:30 · Return to hotels – Overnight stay.

Second day

Friday, September 19 — Sanctuary of Pompei

  • Morning · Wake up, breakfast and departure for the Sanctuary.
  • 09:15 · Gathering, greetings and plenary assembly – Theme: The encounter is joy (Sala Trapani).
  • 12:00 · Lunch (Sala Signora).
  • 13:50 · Activities to choose from: cultural visits (excavations or sanctuary), workshops or games.
  • 19:30 · Dinner (Sala Signora).
  • 21:00 · Great festival with regional gastronomic specialties.
  • 23:00 · Return to hotels – Overnight stay.

Third day

Saturday, September 20 — Sanctuary of Pompei

  • Morning · Wake up, breakfast and departure for the Sanctuary.
  • 09:15 · Gathering, greetings and plenary assembly – Theme: Everything... upside down (Sala Trapani).
  • 13:00 · Lunch (Sala Signora).
  • 14:30 · Return to hotels.
  • 15:30 · Exchange groups in different hotels.
  • Late afternoon · Return to the Sanctuary (time to be agreed).
  • 18:30 · Dinner on time (Sala Signora).
  • 20:00 · Itinerant vigil (courtyard in front of Sala Trapani).
  • 22:30 · Return – Overnight stay.

Fourth day

Sunday, September 21 — Sanctuary of Pompei · Return

  • Morning · Wake up and breakfast. Check-out.
  • 08:30 · Load luggage onto buses.
  • 09:30 · Gathering (Sala Trapani) – Theme: Returning home.
  • 10:00 · Holy Mass.
  • 11:15 · Sending forth and final greetings.
  • 12:00 · Lunch (Sala Signora). For those leaving early: travel basket.
  • 13:00 · Departures and return. End of journey and services.

Organizational note

Times and details may vary for the best conduct of the pilgrimage.

Included / not included

  • The fee includes: travel (bus/train/plane), local transfers, 3 nights in B&B, lunches and dinners at the Sanctuary (catering), pilgrim kit.
  • Does not include: first day lunch, drinks, museum/monument admissions, everything not indicated above.
